Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

OU Estimator ("the App") is a scientific tool for estimating Ornstein-Uhlenbeck parameters from price series data. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data you enter (price series, estimation results, history) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No data is sent to any server or third party.

Data Storage

All computation and data storage happens locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
