# OU Estimator Store Listing

## Positioning

OU Estimator is a technical finance and statistics tool for estimating Ornstein-Uhlenbeck mean reversion parameters from uniformly sampled price series.

Primary audience: quantitative traders, finance students, researchers, data analysts, and developers testing mean reversion assumptions.

Primary keywords: Ornstein-Uhlenbeck, mean reversion, OU estimator, half-life, volatility, theta, mu, sigma, OLS, MLE, price series, quantitative finance.

## Apple App Store

Title, 30 chars: `OU Estimator`

Subtitle, 30 chars: `Mean Reversion Toolkit`

Promotional text, 170 chars: `Estimate theta, mu, sigma, half-life, and fit diagnostics from price series using OLS or exact MLE. Import CSV/TXT and save runs to history.`

Keywords, 99 chars: `ornstein,uhlenbeck,mean,reversion,theta,mu,sigma,halflife,ols,mle,volatility,quant,finance`

Description:

`OU Estimator helps you estimate Ornstein-Uhlenbeck mean reversion parameters from uniformly sampled price series. Paste values, import CSV/TXT, choose OLS or exact MLE, and review theta, mu, sigma, half-life, fit diagnostics, and price chart output in a focused mobile workflow.

Use it for quantitative finance study, strategy research, time-series checks, and quick mean reversion analysis when you need transparent parameters without a spreadsheet setup.

Features:
- OLS estimator for fast AR(1)-based OU parameter estimates
- Exact MLE estimator using pure-Dart Nelder-Mead optimization
- Theta, mu, sigma, half-life, R2, residual standard deviation, log-likelihood, and observation count
- CSV/TXT import plus manual price series entry
- History for saved runs, reload, rename, and delete
- JSON export and native share sheet support
- Dark interface built for repeated technical use

OU Estimator is designed for educational, research, and analytical workflows. It does not provide investment advice.`

## Google Play

Title, 12/50 chars: `OU Estimator`

Full description:

`Estimate Ornstein-Uhlenbeck mean reversion parameters from uniformly sampled price series.

OU Estimator gives quantitative finance students, analysts, traders, and researchers a focused way to calculate theta, mu, sigma, half-life, and fit diagnostics from price data.

Key features:

- Export JSON and share results through native OS sharing

Useful for:
- Mean reversion analysis
- OU model parameter estimation
- Quantitative finance coursework
- Strategy research and backtesting prep
- Time-series diagnostics

OU Estimator is a research and education tool. It does not provide financial advice.`
